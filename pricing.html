Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in the Free plan?
The Free plan gives you unlimited access to all core features (EVM analysis, risk detection, DCMA audit, S-curves) with 2 XER file uploads. Perfect for testing the platform and evaluating your projects.
Can I upgrade from Free to a paid plan?
Yes! You can upgrade anytime. If you're on Free and exceed 2 uploads, simply upgrade to Starter or Professional and continue working. No data loss.
What's the difference between Starter and Professional?
Starter includes HTML export only. Professional adds Excel export, priority email support, and advanced reporting features. Choose based on your export needs and team size.
Do you offer annual billing discounts?
Contact our sales team to discuss volume discounts, annual billing options, and enterprise arrangements. We're happy to work with your budget.
Is there a trial period?
Yes! The Free plan is your trial. Get full access to core features with 2 XER uploads, no credit card required. Upgrade whenever you're ready.
What happens to my data if I downgrade?
Your data stays safe. If you downgrade from Professional to Starter, you'll still have access to all your previous analyses. You just won't be able to create new Excel exports.
